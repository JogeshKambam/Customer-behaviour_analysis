#  Customer Behaviour Analysis

A data analytics project that explores customer purchasing patterns using **SQL**, **Python (Jupyter Notebook)**, and **Power BI**.
The goal is to understand revenue trends, product preferences, discount usage, and customer segments.

---

##  Project Files

* `customer_behaviour.sql` – Data cleaning + analysis queries
* `Customer_Shopping_Behaviour_Analysis notebook.ipynb` – Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
* `customer_behaviour.pbix` – Power BI dashboard
* Project report (PDF) and presentation (PPT)

---

##  Objectives

* Analyze customer behaviour and spending patterns
* Identify high-value customer segments
* Study the impact of discounts, reviews, and shipping types
* Generate insights for business decision-making

---

##  Tools Used

* **SQL** – Data cleaning & transformations
* **Python (Jupyter Notebook)** – Exploratory Data Analysis
* **Power BI** – Interactive dashboard & data visualization

---

##  Summary of Analysis

* Cleaned and standardized dataset using SQL
* Performed EDA to understand distributions and trends
* Segmented customers (New, Returning, Loyal)
* Compared revenue by gender, age groups, and categories
* Identified top-rated and most purchased products
* Analyzed subscription and discount behaviour

---

##  Key Insights

* Subscribed customers spend more on average
* Young Adults (26–35) contribute the highest revenue
* High-rated items often align with top-selling products
* Express shipping customers tend to spend more
* Some products rely heavily on discounts

---

##  Recommendations

* Personalize offers for Returning & Loyal customers
* Promote best-rated, high-performing products
* Optimize discount policies to protect margins
* Target key age groups with focused marketing
